Meet Dr. Arivan Moodley

Dr. Arivan Moodley is a highly experienced General Practitioner serving the Chatsworth and greater Durban community. With qualifications from UDW and MEDUNSA, he brings decades of clinical expertise and a personal touch to every consultation.

Recognized with the prestigious Best of Durban Award as the 3rd Best Family Doctor in Durban, Dr. Moodley is committed to delivering comprehensive, patient-centred care for individuals and families of all ages.
